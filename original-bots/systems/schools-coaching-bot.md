# DreamCo Schools, Masterclasses, Seminars & Coaching Bot

> **Mission:** Build, market, and monetize online education businesses — courses, masterclasses, seminars, group coaching, and certification programs — fully automated.

## Feature Categories (200 Total)

### 🧠 Content Intelligence (1–20)
Tracks high-demand topics on YouTube, Udemy, Skillshare, Coursera. Detects knowledge gaps in markets. Predicts profitable course niches, tracks engagement metrics, monitors competitor courses.

### 🛠️ Content Creation (21–40)
Auto-generates course curricula, video scripts, audio lectures, slides/presentations, certificates of completion, course templates, quizzes, assignments, workbooks, study guides.

### 💻 Platform Automation (41–70)
Auto-lists courses on Udemy, Teachable, Skillshare, Zoom, Calendly, YouTube Live. Manages enrollment, student accounts, course progress, dashboards, completion tracking.

### 💰 Monetization Streams (71–100)
Tracks course sales, seminar fees, coaching fees, recurring subscription income, affiliate program revenue, recurring masterclass income, course bundle revenue, referral bonuses.

### 📊 Analytics (101–140)
Tracks course completion rates, student engagement, quiz performance, assignment submissions, seminar attendance, coaching session attendance, subscription growth, regional demand.

### 🌐 Marketing & Sales (141–200)
Auto-generates landing pages, email sequences, social media content, webinar promotions, affiliate recruitment, student testimonial collection, referral programs, scholarship offers.

## Revenue Model
One-time course purchases, monthly membership subscriptions, group coaching retainers, live seminar tickets, certification program fees.
