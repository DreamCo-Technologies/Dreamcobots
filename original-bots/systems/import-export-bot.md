# DreamCo Import/Export, Travel & Global Business Bot

> **Mission:** Automate global import/export operations, travel services, supplier management, and residual income streams from worldwide commerce.

## Feature Categories (200 Total)

### 🧠 Market Intelligence (1–10)
Tracks trending products globally, supplier reliability, shipping cost trends, customs regulations, import/export restrictions, tariff rates, profitable product niches, high-demand items, competitor strategies, regional logistics trends.

### 🛠️ Supplier & Product Automation (11–30)
Auto-sources worldwide suppliers, verifies authenticity, compares pricing/shipping, monitors product quality, updates catalogs, negotiates contracts, tracks lead times, manages order quantities, tracks product lifecycle.

### 💻 Logistics & Shipping (31–60)
Auto-generates shipping labels, calculates shipping costs, selects fastest/cheapest methods, tracks shipments globally, manages customs clearance, generates import/export documentation, updates delivery estimates, notifies clients, optimizes multi-leg shipments.

### 💰 Revenue Streams (61–100)
Import/export profits, shipping service income, recurring trade contracts, global product bundles, travel package revenue, consulting service income, multi-platform revenue tracking.

### ✈️ Travel Services (101–150)
Auto-books flights, hotels, tours; creates travel packages; manages group bookings; tracks travel trends; finds arbitrage in travel pricing; manages travel affiliate programs.

### 🌐 Global Business Expansion (151–200)
Identifies new markets, manages international partnerships, navigates trade agreements, tracks tariff opportunities, manages currency conversion, oversees global compliance.

## Revenue Model
Trade margin, logistics service fees, travel package commissions, consulting retainers, international partnership royalties.
