# DreamCo Penny Stock, Trading & Investment Bot

> **Mission:** Automate penny stock trading, crypto, investments, and all forms of financial residual income while tracking high-return opportunities.

## Feature Categories (200 Total)

### 🧠 Market Intelligence (1–20)
Tracks penny stocks daily, top ETFs, mutual funds, crypto/forex markets. Detects trending stocks, high volatility assets, insider trading signals, short interest, trending sectors.

### 📊 Analytics & Forecasting (21–40)
Auto-calculates moving averages, RSI, MACD. Tracks historical stock/crypto data. Forecasts price trends, volatility, dividend payouts, split announcements. Generates risk reports.

### 💰 Automated Trading (41–70)
Auto-buys/sells: penny stocks, ETFs, mutual funds, crypto, foreign currencies, options, futures, bonds, REITs.

### 🔗 Broker & Exchange Integration (71–100)
Integrates Robinhood, E*TRADE, TD Ameritrade, Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Forex platforms, Interactive Brokers. Tracks portfolio performance, auto-syncs trade history.

### 🤖 AI Self-Learning (101–130)
Learns high-performing strategies, high-volatility opportunities, market timing, sector trends, risk management, optimal buy/sell points, arbitrage opportunities, recurring high-ROI trades.

### 📈 Portfolio Management (131–170)
Tracks portfolio diversification, rebalances automatically, manages position sizing, applies tax-loss harvesting, tracks dividend reinvestment, manages stop-losses, calculates Sharpe ratio.

### 💸 Residual Income Optimization (171–200)
Manages dividend income streams, crypto staking rewards, bond coupon payments, covered call income, P2P lending income, REIT distributions.

## Revenue Model
Trading profits, dividend income, staking rewards, portfolio management fees, investment signal subscriptions.
