# DreamCo Contracts, Legal Documents, eBooks & Digital Products Bot

> **Mission:** Automate creation, protection, and monetization of legal documents, intellectual property, eBooks, and all digital products.

## Feature Categories (200 Total)

### ⚖️ Legal Document Creation (1–30)
Auto-generates business contracts, NDAs, partnership agreements, service agreements, SaaS terms of service, privacy policies, licensing agreements, copyright contracts, patents, trademark filings, LLC formation documents, operating agreements.

### 📚 eBook & Digital Product Creation (31–70)
Auto-generates eBook content, workbooks, guides & manuals, templates, worksheets, online courses, digital planners, checklists, video tutorials, audiobooks.

### 💻 Distribution & Sales (71–110)
Auto-lists eBooks on Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, Udemy, Creative Market, Audible. Manages pricing, discounts, product listings, inventory, downloads.

### 💰 Monetization (111–150)
Tracks eBook revenue, course revenue, template sales, licensing fees, royalty payments, recurring subscription income, digital product bundles, affiliate commissions. Forecasts lifetime income per asset.

### 🔒 IP Protection (151–180)
Monitors unauthorized use of content globally, auto-files DMCA takedowns, tracks trademark infringement, manages copyright portfolios, tracks patent status.

### 📊 Analytics & Optimization (181–200)
Tracks bestselling product categories, regional demand, pricing optimization, refund rates, conversion rates per platform.

## Revenue Model
Direct sales, licensing fees, royalty collections, legal document subscription service, digital product bundles.
