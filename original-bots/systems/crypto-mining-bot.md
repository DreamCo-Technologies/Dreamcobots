# DreamCo Crypto Mining, Blockchain & Wallet Management Bot

> **Mission:** Automate all aspects of cryptocurrency mining, wallet management, staking, DeFi, NFTs, and blockchain income for maximum passive returns.

## Feature Categories (200 Total)

### ⛏️ Mining Operations (1–20)
Auto-detects most profitable coins to mine, auto-switches mining pools, auto-calculates profitability, auto-maintains mining hardware health, auto-updates mining software.

### 💳 Wallet Management (21–40)
Auto-generates wallets, manages multiple crypto wallets, tracks balances/transaction history/recurring payments/staking rewards/yield farming returns/NFT ownership/smart contract interactions/DeFi investments.

### 💰 Monetization & Income Streams (41–80)
Tracks mining profits, staking income, DeFi income, NFT sales, crypto trading profits. Auto-expands residual income streams. Manages yield farming protocols, liquidity provision, arbitrage across exchanges.

### 📊 Analytics & Optimization (81–120)
Tracks coin performance, wallet ROI, NFT trends, staking yields, DeFi returns, mining profitability. Predicts future trends, tracks global market performance, auto-generates optimization strategies.

### 🔗 Multi-Platform Integration (121–160)
Integrates Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Uniswap, PancakeSwap, Aave, Compound, OpenSea, Rarible, MetaMask.

### 🔒 Security & Compliance (161–200)
Multi-signature wallets, cold storage management, tax reporting automation, AML/KYC compliance, wallet encryption, seed phrase backup systems, hardware wallet integration.

## Revenue Model
Mining rewards, staking yields, DeFi interest, NFT flip margins, crypto arbitrage spreads.
