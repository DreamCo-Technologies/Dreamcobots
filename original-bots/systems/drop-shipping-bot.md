# DreamCo Drop Shipping, Affiliate Marketing & E-commerce Bot

> **Mission:** Automate product sourcing, drop shipping, affiliate marketing, and e-commerce for global residual income.

## Feature Categories (200 Total)

### 🧠 Market Intelligence (1–10)
Tracks trending products on Amazon, Alibaba, Temu, Shopify, Etsy. Identifies high-margin, low-competition products. Detects viral product patterns, predicts seasonal demand.

### 🛠️ Product Sourcing Automation (11–30)
Auto-sources from Alibaba, Amazon, Temu, eBay, Etsy. Compares supplier prices/shipping, verifies reliability/quality/reviews. Tracks inventory levels at suppliers.

### 💻 Store Management (31–60)
Auto-creates Shopify, WooCommerce, Etsy stores. Auto-lists products, manages inventory, syncs stock levels, updates prices, applies promotions, creates bundles, manages abandoned carts.

### 💰 Monetization (61–100)
Tracks affiliate commissions, drop shipping profit, recurring subscription revenue, multi-platform sales. Auto-expands highest-profit product lines.

### 📣 Marketing Automation (101–150)
Auto-generates social media ads, email campaigns, influencer outreach, product reviews, SEO content. Manages Google Shopping feeds, Facebook/Instagram/TikTok ads.

### 📊 Analytics (151–200)
Tracks conversion rates per platform, profit margins per product, supplier performance, customer LTV, campaign ROI, seasonal trends, global expansion opportunities.

## Revenue Model
Product margin (10-40%), affiliate commissions (5-30%), subscription product fees, white-label store management services.
