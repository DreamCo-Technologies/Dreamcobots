# DreamCo Autonomous Consulting & Digital Agency Bot

> **Mission:** Fully automated consulting and digital agency that builds brands, runs ads, creates content, manages clients, and generates high-ticket and residual income.

## Feature Categories (200 Total)

### 🧠 Market Intelligence (1–20)
Tracks trending consulting niches, profitable digital services, competitor service pricing, high-demand client industries. Detects gaps in service offerings. Tracks ROI of ad campaigns, conversion rates per service.

### 🛠️ Service Automation (21–50)
Auto-generates client proposals, contracts, invoices, service packages, pricing models, project timelines, deliverable templates, onboarding documents, strategy guides, branding guidelines.

### 📣 Content & Marketing (51–90)
Auto-generates social media posts, ad copy, email campaigns, landing pages, sales funnels, blogs, video scripts, webinars, newsletters, case studies.

### 💰 Monetization (91–130)
Tracks client subscription payments, retainer contracts, recurring project income, affiliate revenue, ad revenue from campaigns. Forecasts client lifetime value. Auto-expands service tiers, identifies upsell opportunities, generates high-ticket packages.

### 📊 Analytics (131–160)
Tracks ad performance, client engagement metrics, conversion rates, email open rates, website traffic, content performance, social media engagement.

### 🌐 Multi-Platform Integration (161–200)
Integrates with 50+ marketing platforms: HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Hootsuite, Buffer, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads, Canva, Adobe Creative, Notion, Asana.

## Revenue Model
Monthly retainers ($2,500-$10,000/client), project fees, performance-based ad management (10-15% ad spend), white-label agency licensing.
