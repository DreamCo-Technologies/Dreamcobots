# Bot Founder App Store

DreamCo treats every bot as a supervised business owner preparing an app, website, workflow, simulation, course, or service for the DreamCo app store.

The goal is not blind autonomy. The goal is disciplined founder behavior: study the market, find problems, design an app, package an offer, prepare marketing, create a sandbox demo, and ask for approval before any live action.

## What Every Bot Prepares

- Competitor study plan.
- Customer problem brief.
- Autonomous app concept.
- MVP scope.
- Revenue model.
- Marketing plan.
- Customer discovery plan.
- App-store listing.
- Sandbox test plan.
- Deployment plan.
- Daily learning loop.
- Approval gates.

## Study Labs

- Competition Lab: public competitor research, pricing, feature gaps, reviews, positioning, and substitutes.
- Autonomous Money Lab: ethical value creation, pricing tests, offer stacks, cost savings, and revenue recovery.
- App Builder Lab: app concept, screen map, API plan, sandbox tests, deployment plan, and rollback plan.
- Company Builder Lab: prospectus, landing page, KPIs, support process, and daily review.
- Marketing Lab: content, SEO, ad drafts, launch plans, demo scripts, and audience segments.
- Customer Discovery Lab: customer segments, interview questions, lead research packets, and validation plans.
- App Store Packaging Lab: listing, proof, pricing card, risk label, and owner approval queue.

## Live Action Rules

Bots may research, draft, score, package, simulate, and propose.

Bots must not do these without owner approval:

- Contact customers.
- Run ads.
- Spend money.
- Move money.
- Deploy publicly.
- Publish app-store listings.
- Post to social media.
- Send email, SMS, or phone outreach.
- Make legal, medical, financial, or income claims.
- Collect personal data beyond approved lead records.
- Change third-party accounts.

## Commands

Generate the report:

```bash
npm run report:bot-founder-store
```

Check the committed report:

```bash
npm run check:bot-founder-store
```
