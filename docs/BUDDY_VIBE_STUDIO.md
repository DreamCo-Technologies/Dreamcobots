# Buddy Vibe Studio

Buddy Vibe Studio is the client-facing build lane for turning plain-language ideas into apps, bots, music video packets, kids learning videos, college courses, video games, simulations, and client business systems.

## What clients can ask for

- App or bot: working prototype, bot prospectus, APIs, webhooks, sandbox checks, and a demo page.
- Music video: concept, storyboard, shot list, scene prompts, edit timeline, and rights checklist.
- Kids learning video: age-safe script, visuals, narration, quiz, captions, and parent or teacher notes.
- College course: syllabus, modules, lessons, assignments, rubrics, labs, slides, and outcomes.
- Video game: design doc, playable prototype, rules, levels, scoring, save state, QA, and deployment path.
- Simulation: business, science, finance, real estate, operations, training, or emergency scenarios with metrics and controls.

## Default build flow

1. Prompt: client describes the outcome, audience, style, budget, and limits.
2. Packet: Buddy creates the design packet, asset plan, build steps, and test checklist.
3. Prototype: Buddy builds the first sandbox version or production packet.
4. Review: client approves rights, safety, claims, age fit, brand fit, and release path.
5. Ship: Buddy prepares files, pull request, hosted demo, or client handoff.

## Voice and image rules

Buddy must be stricter than broad social-platform defaults:

- No cloning a real person voice, face, likeness, or image without explicit written consent.
- Always label AI-generated or AI-edited media in the asset record.
- No political, election, financial, medical, legal, emergency, or public-safety impersonation.
- No cloning minors.
- No sexual or degrading fake media of real people.
- No fake customer testimonials, fake founders, fake employees, fake endorsements, or fake authority figures.
- No robocalls or automated outreach using cloned voices.
- Every generated voice or image asset must store consent proof, source, creator, timestamp, and usage rights.

## Product stance

Buddy should compete by being a trust-first creative systems builder, not only a website generator. The client sees a guided production studio; the repository keeps the safety rules, generated assets, tests, and approval evidence.
