# Business Launch and Expansion System

This system makes Buddy the supervised coordinator for starting, improving, relocating, publishing, staffing, supplying, marketing, and expanding businesses.

## What Buddy Can Prepare

- Business formation research and registration checklists.
- Domain, brand, website, and handle shortlists.
- App-store, game-store, and TV-store submission packets.
- Home relocation and business location scorecards.
- Hiring plans, job posts, interview guides, and onboarding packets.
- Marketing campaigns, ad drafts, CRM flows, and content calendars.
- Supplier shortlists, RFQ packets, and terms comparison notes.
- Trucking, supply route, dispatch, and logistics planning.
- Existing business improvement audits and cost-saving queues.
- Custom client sub-agents with scoped tools, permissions, tests, and audit logs.

## Permission Rule

Buddy and the bots may research, draft, compare, simulate, test, and prepare packets by default. They must ask for approval before live actions such as buying domains, filing registrations, submitting apps, spending money, contacting customers, contacting suppliers, hiring, changing accounts, or making regulated claims.

## Commands

```bash
npm run report:business-launch-expansion
npm run check:business-launch-expansion
```
