# Bot Contract Discovery System

Every DreamCo bot should continuously search for contracts and opportunity paths that match its division, capabilities, proof, client type, and business model.

## What Bots Can Do By Default

- Search public and owner-approved sources.
- Summarize contract, grant, loan, RFP, vendor, supplier, trucking route, construction, procurement, app-store, and partnership opportunities.
- Cover federal, state, county, city, school, university, public authority, nonprofit, private, supplier, and subcontracting paths.
- Score fit, risk, deadline, proof needed, eligibility, required documents, cash-flow impact, competition, and value.
- Prepare response checklists and approval packets.
- Create weekly owner/client opportunity digests.

## What Requires Approval

Bots must ask before submitting bids, filing grant or loan applications, registering vendor accounts, contacting buyers, contacting suppliers, contacting carriers, signing contracts, accepting terms, spending money, sharing financial or client data, uploading certification documents, or making price commitments.

## Opportunity Families

- Public contracts: federal, state, county, city, public authority, school district, university, tribal, and public-private partnership work.
- Private contracts: private RFPs, professional services, technology, data, construction, maintenance, security, staffing, marketing, and creative services.
- Grants: federal, state, local, foundation, workforce, innovation, research, education, health, agriculture, clean energy, and disaster recovery grants.
- Loans and financing: small business loans, microloans, equipment financing, working capital, real estate, construction, export/import, revenue-based financing, and lines of credit.
- Procurement and supply: vendor registration, supplier diversity registration, prime/subcontractor matching, supplier contracts, logistics, freight, warehouse, fulfillment, and route contracts.
- Commercial growth: app-store services, enterprise software, affiliate, reseller, licensing, partnership, and client-service opportunities.

## Commands

```bash
npm run report:contract-discovery
npm run check:contract-discovery
```
