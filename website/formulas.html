🏠 70% Rule

Free

Maximum purchase price for a flip = (ARV × 70%) − Repair Costs. Never overpay for a property.

Max Purchase Price
= (ARV × 0.70) − Repair Costs
e.g. ($300k × 0.70) − $35k = $175k max

💰 Rule of 72

Free

Estimate when an investment doubles. Divide 72 by the annual interest rate to get years to double.

Years to Double
= 72 ÷ Annual Rate (%)
e.g. 72 ÷ 8% = 9 years to double

🎯 Customer LTV

Pro

Lifetime Value of a customer. Use to determine max allowable customer acquisition cost (CAC).

LTV
= Avg Order Value × Purchase Freq × Lifespan
Max CAC = LTV ÷ 3 (for 3:1 LTV:CAC ratio)

₿ DeFi APY vs APR

Pro

APY accounts for compounding, APR does not. Always compare APY to understand true DeFi yields.

APY from APR
= (1 + APR/n)^n − 1
n = compounding periods per year

🚗 Car Flip ROI

Free

Net profit percentage on a car flip. Includes all-in costs: purchase, recondition, transport, and fees.

Car Flip ROI
= (Sell − All-in Cost) ÷ All-in Cost × 100
Grade A+ = ROI > 20% · Grade D = ROI < 5%
