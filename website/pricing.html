Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "bot"? ↓

Each specialized AI is one bot. A bot has a unique system prompt, skill set, division, and tier. The 5 free-tier bots are yours to choose from any of the 1,051+ available.

Can I change my plan anytime? ↓

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. Prorated billing applies to upgrades. No long-term contracts on Pro or Enterprise.

What is "Full Autonomy"? ↓

Full Autonomy lets your bots operate 24/7 without needing your approval for each action. They generate leads, analyze deals, write content, and run DeFi strategies automatically.

Does Buddy Bot require a separate plan? ↓

Buddy Bot is available on all plans, but full ELITE capabilities (Agent mode, Voice, GitHub, Security Scan) require Elite tier. Basic chat with Buddy is included in Free.

What payment methods do you accept? ↓

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe, plus Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, Solana, and 11 other cryptocurrencies.
