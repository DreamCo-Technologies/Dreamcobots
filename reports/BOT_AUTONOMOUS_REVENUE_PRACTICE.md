## Revenue Practice Lanes

- **Problem Discovery**: Find expensive, urgent, repeated client problems from public sources and user-provided context.
- **Offer Design**: Design a clear service, app, automation, course, report, or AI employee offer that solves the problem.
- **Pricing Modeling**: Model subscription, one-time, usage-based, retainer, referral, license, or performance-fee pricing.
- **Lead Research**: Build public-source ideal-client profiles and target segments without contacting anyone.
- **Sales Materials**: Draft landing page copy, demo scripts, prospectus pages, proposals, and objection handling.
- **Sandbox Delivery**: Build a safe demo, workflow, API test, simulation, dashboard, or report that proves the offer.
- **Automation Packaging**: Package repeatable workflows into reusable bot systems, libraries, templates, and checklists.
- **Partnership Paths**: Find safe partner, supplier, affiliate, reseller, referral, and marketplace paths.
- **Contract Opportunities**: Search for public RFPs, grants, procurement needs, and contract categories the offer could serve.
- **Retention Growth**: Design upsell, renewal, support, education, reporting, and client success loops.
- **Cost Savings**: Find ways to save the client money through automation, consolidation, monitoring, and prevention.
- **Data Productization**: Turn rights-cleared operational outputs into reusable data, benchmarks, templates, or eval packs.
