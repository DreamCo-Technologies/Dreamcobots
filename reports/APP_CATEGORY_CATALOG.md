## Task Screen

- Buddy recommends apps by task fit, user preference fit, risk, price, permissions, data export, reviews, automation support, and source freshness.
- Buddy may compare sports betting apps as apps, but must not place bets or present gambling picks as guaranteed outcomes.
- Buddy may compare coupon apps and prepare redemption steps, but redemption, purchase, account connection, or payment actions require approval.
- Buddy must support a custom user app list so clients can compare only the apps they already use or trust.
- Buddy can prepare one-app or multi-app workflows, but external execution happens only through approved integrations and approval gates.
