## Safe Autonomous Lanes

- repo_health: Keep code quality, imports, generated catalogs, reports, and dashboard routes continuously checked.
- bot_buildout: Move every bot toward a runnable local-first service with prospectus, capabilities, sandbox, workflow, and test evidence.
- app_and_simulation_factory: Prepare apps, websites, games, courses, simulations, media packets, and business-system packets for Buddy or clients.
- all_app_task_control: Let Buddy help with tasks across apps by preparing visible, reversible, approval-ready steps.
- secret_access_management: Safely manage access requirements without exposing or committing secret values.
- money_opportunity_research: Find useful, lawful, low-cost opportunities and prepare reviewed action packets.

## Approval Wall

- submit signups
- accept terms
- grant repository, cloud, payment, email, or social access
- create or change live credentials
- spend, move, refund, charge, trade, or reconcile money
- send outreach, messages, calls, proposals, bids, or social posts
- buy domains
- publish websites, apps, games, store listings, or marketplace listings
- collect private or sensitive people data
- make legal, medical, financial, employment, tenant, credit, identity, or public-safety decisions
- delete files, overwrite user work, or run destructive commands
