# Buddy Life Opportunity Encyclopedia

Give Buddy a friendly, device-ready opportunity encyclopedia for personal life, business life, and work life so clients can find legitimate money, savings, jobs, funding, contracts, benefits, and productivity paths with clear approval and evidence gates.

## Summary

- Resource lanes: 8
- Bot coverage: 1248
- Friend copilot steps: 6
- Device surfaces: 10
- Approval gates: 11

## Resource Lanes

### Jobs And Careers

Help clients find jobs, improve resumes, match qualifications, track applications, and prepare interview packets.

- Safe actions: search public job boards, compare role fit, draft resume bullets, prepare interview plan
- Approval required: submit_application, message_recruiter, upload_resume, share_personal_data

### Side Hustles

Find realistic side-income paths, starter checklists, price tests, marketplace fit, and risk notes.

- Safe actions: compare public demand, draft offer, estimate startup cost, prepare first task checklist
- Approval required: contact_customer, publish_offer, buy_supplies, accept_terms

### Contracts Grants Procurement

Find contracts, grants, RFPs, vendor registrations, procurement paths, and bid-readiness requirements.

- Safe actions: search public portals, score fit, summarize requirements, prepare bid checklist
- Approval required: submit_bid, submit_grant, register_vendor, contact_buyer, sign_contract

### Investors Loans Credit

Help clients prepare investor lists, loan readiness, grant alternatives, credit-building plans, and home-loan preparation.

- Safe actions: prepare readiness checklist, compare public program requirements, draft pitch packet, model affordability
- Approval required: submit_loan_application, contact_investor, connect_bank, pull_credit, apply_for_credit_card

### Unclaimed Money And Estates

Help clients search official public unclaimed property, probate, estate, genealogy, and beneficiary research paths.

- Safe actions: prepare official-source checklist, draft family-tree research plan, summarize claim requirements, organize proof documents
- Approval required: submit_claim, upload_identity_documents, contact_court, contact_relative, pay_researcher

### Settlements And Claims

Track public settlement and claims notices weekly and help users identify possible eligibility without creating false claims.

- Safe actions: summarize public settlement notices, list eligibility questions, prepare document checklist, flag deadline
- Approval required: submit_claim, state_eligibility_as_fact, upload_documents, contact_law_firm

### Associations And Networks

Maintain association, chamber, trade group, union, incubator, accelerator, mentor, alumni, and professional network resources for every bot category.

- Safe actions: search public association directories, map membership benefits, prepare outreach draft, score fit
- Approval required: join_association, pay_dues, send_outreach, share_profile

### Treasure And Asset Research

Help clients organize lawful research into collectibles, inherited assets, mineral rights, property records, forgotten accounts, and historical records.

- Safe actions: prepare research checklist, summarize public records, organize provenance notes, draft appraisal packet
- Approval required: enter_private_property, pay_appraiser, submit_claim, contact_owner_or_heir

## Safety Note

Buddy can research, organize, score, and prepare packets. Buddy must not create false claims, guarantee money, submit applications, contact outside parties, or provide legal/financial/credit decisions without approval and review.
