## Live Action Policy

Bots may research, draft, score, package, simulate, and propose. Bots must not contact customers, run ads, spend money, deploy publicly, move money, create fake claims, or publish app-store listings without owner approval.

## Study Loops

### Competition Lab

Study public competitors, substitutes, pricing, feature gaps, positioning, onboarding, reviews, and customer complaints.

- Outputs: competitor_map, feature_gap_matrix, pricing_notes, review_pain_points, differentiation_angle

### Autonomous Money Lab

Find ethical ways the bot can create value, package offers, price services, reduce costs, recover revenue, or help customers make better decisions.

- Outputs: revenue_model, offer_stack, pricing_test, unit_economics_notes, risk_disclosures

### App Builder Lab

Design one app-store-ready product for the bot with a clear user, core workflow, demo, sandbox test, and deployment plan.

- Outputs: app_concept, mvp_scope, screen_map, api_contracts, sandbox_tests, deployment_plan, rollback_plan

### Company Builder Lab

Prepare each bot as a supervised AI company with a prospectus, website plan, operating model, KPIs, support process, and improvement loop.

- Outputs: company_prospectus, landing_page_brief, operating_runbook, kpi_scorecard, support_playbook, daily_review

### Marketing Lab

Draft ethical advertising, content, SEO, demos, launch copy, and campaign experiments for owner review.

- Outputs: audience_segments, message_tests, content_calendar, ad_drafts, demo_script, launch_checklist

### Customer Discovery Lab

Find customer problems, draft interview questions, score segments, and prepare lead records from public, compliant sources.

- Outputs: problem_brief, customer_segments, interview_questions, lead_research_packet, validation_plan

### App Store Packaging Lab

Package each bot app with screenshots or mockups, prospectus copy, pricing, proof, sandbox demo, approval status, and risk labels.

- Outputs: store_listing, prospectus_page, demo_packet, pricing_card, test_evidence, risk_label, approval_queue
