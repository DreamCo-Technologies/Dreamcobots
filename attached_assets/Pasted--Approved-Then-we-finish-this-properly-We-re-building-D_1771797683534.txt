Formula 1: Extreme Discount Score

Deal Score =
(Discount % × 0.4) +
(Historical Low Factor × 0.2) +
(Resell ROI × 0.3) –
(Risk Score × 0.1)

Formula 2: Flip Margin Formula

Net Profit =
(Sold Price – Fees – Shipping – Tax – Time Cost)
